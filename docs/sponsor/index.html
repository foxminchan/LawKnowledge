Sponsor

Law Knowledge is an MIT licensed open-source project and completely free to use. The he tremendous amount of effort needed to maintain such a large ecosystem and develop new features for the project is only made sustainable thanks to the generous financial backing of our sponsors.

Why sponsor us?​

By sponsoring the Law Knowledge, you are contributing to the improvement of the application and helping to ensure its long-term sustainability. Your sponsorship will help us to continue to develop and maintain the application, provide support to users, and keep the application secure and up-to-date.

  • Continued Development: Your contributions fund ongoing development efforts, allowing us to add new features, enhance performance, and improve user experience.

  • Community Support: Sponsorship helps us provide timely assistance to users, address issues, and respond to feature requests through our support channels.

  • Documentation and Training: We can create comprehensive documentation and educational materials to help users make the most of the application.

  • Bug Fixes and Security: Your sponsorship helps ensure the application remains secure and free from critical issues.

How to Sponsor?​

Sponsorships can be done via GitHub Sponsors, Kofi, Patreon or Buy Me a Coffee. You can choose to sponsor us monthly or yearly. We appreciate any amount you can give, and we will make sure your sponsorship goes a long way. Invoices can be obtained via GitHub's payment system. Both monthly-recurring sponsorships and one-time donations are accepted. Recurring sponsorships are entitled to logo placements as specified in Sponsorship Tiers.

If you have any questions or require further information about sponsorship, please feel free to contact our sponsorship team at nguyenxuannhan407@gmail.com.

Sponsor as a Business​

Does your company use Law Knowledge? Do you want to help us continue to develop and maintain the application? Then consider sponsoring us. Your logo will be displayed on the Law Knowledge website and in the application itself. You can also choose to sponsor us anonymously.

The Law Knowledge is used by thousands of companies and organizations around the world. By sponsoring us, you are helping to ensure the long-term sustainability of the application and the continued development of new features.

Sponsor as an Individual​

If you are an individual user and have enjoyed the productivity of using Law Knowledge, consider donating as a sign of appreciation - like buying us coffee once in a while. Many of our team members accept sponsorships and donations via GitHub Sponsors.

You can also choose to sponsor us anonymously.

Sponsorship Tiers​

We offer five tiers of sponsorship:

  • 🥇 Gold (VND 10,000,000/month):

    • Logo placement on the Law Knowledge website.
    • Logo placement in the README of the Law Knowledge repository.

  • 🥈 Silver (VND 5,000,000/month):

    • Logo placement on the Law Knowledge website.
    • Logo placement in the README of the Law Knowledge repository.

  • 🥉 Bronze (VND 2,000,000/month):

    • Logo placement in the README of the Law Knowledge repository.

  • 🏅 Supporter (VND 1,000,000/month):

    • Logo placement in the README of the Law Knowledge repository.

  • 🍺 Generous Backer (VND 100,000/month):

    • An email from us thanking you for your support.
